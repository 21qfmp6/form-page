About

Who we are and what we do.

OUR COMMUNITY

We are deeply rooted in Prince Edward County. We live just up the road at Edwin County Farms where we grow and harvest many of the organic ingredients we serve in our restaurants. Our farm-to-table ethos extends throughout the hotel, encompassing our rich community of artisans and producers, friends whose talent, passion and hard work are woven throughout everything we do. Our commitment to sustainably supporting this land and its people is what drives our sense of purpose..

Always there for you

Dating back to 1879, The Royal has been lovingly restored by a local family, reimagined for today’s traveller while honouring its strong history in the community. A stone’s throw from picturesque wineries, charming restaurants and idyllic natural attractions, The Royal is the ideal jumping-off point for exploring everything that makes The County special. Join us on Picton Main Street and discover why we love to call The County home..

Stay

The Royal is housed in two neighbouring buildings: The Hotel, our classic accommodations exuding warmth and sensuality, and The Annex, which has roomy, Scandi-inspired studios designed for extended stays. We have 33 guest rooms, including adjacent suites perfectly suited for gathering families or groups of friends, as well as a peaceful library, a swimming pool and a private terrace reserved exclusively for our overnight guests..

spa & gym

Enjoy a massage, facial treatment and more at our spa, all part of a full-service wellness club that includes a sauna and a gym..
